# No Vanity 33 Mission

## Tagline
Wear the Word. Live the Gospel.

## Mission Statement
No Vanity 33 exists to glorify Jesus Christ through apparel that points people to the Gospel. Every design is created to start conversations about Christ, encourage believers, and remind us that our identity is found in Him — not in ourselves.

We are committed to using 40% of profits to help people in need through food, clothing, housing assistance, and acts of service.

## Core Values
- Christ Above All
- Scripture Before Style
- Excellence in Everything
- Integrity Always
- Serve Others First
