# Crimson Worm Tee

Price: $25

Scripture: Psalm 22:6

## Description
A teaching tee based on Psalm 22:6 and the crimson worm picture.

## Why This Design?
Psalm 22:6 says, 'But I am a worm, and no man.' The crimson worm has often been used as an illustration of sacrifice, scarlet covering, and new life — pointing to the Gospel of Christ.
