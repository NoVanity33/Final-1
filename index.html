Our Mission

Why No Vanity 33 Exists

No Vanity 33 was founded with one purpose: to point people toward Christ.

In a world that often celebrates self, appearance, and status, we want every design to remind people that true identity is not found in ourselves. It is found in Jesus Christ.

Every design includes Scripture because God’s Word can reach someone in a moment we may never see. A verse on the back of a shirt may be exactly what someone needed to read that day.

“If you need prayer, just ask me.” Those words are not just a slogan. They are an invitation.

As No Vanity 33 grows, our desire is to use the business to serve people in practical ways and reflect Christ’s love through both words and actions.

Wear your faith. Live with purpose. Point others to Christ.
