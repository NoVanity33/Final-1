THE STORY

Our Mission

No Vanity 33 was founded with one purpose: to point people toward Christ.

In a world that often celebrates self, appearance, and status, we want every design to remind people that true identity is not found in ourselves. It is found in Jesus Christ. The name No Vanity is a reminder that our lives are meant to reflect Him, not ourselves.

Every shirt is created with intention. Our designs are meant to start conversations, encourage faith, and remind both the person wearing them and the people around them that hope is still alive.

Why Every Shirt Carries Scripture

One of the things that makes No Vanity 33 unique is that every design includes Scripture. Rather than printing the same verses repeatedly, we intentionally use different passages from the Bible across our apparel. Our hope is that every order places God’s Word into someone’s life in a fresh way. A verse on the back of a shirt may be exactly what someone needed to read on an ordinary day.

If You Need Prayer, Just Ask Me

You will also notice a simple message connected to our apparel: “If you need prayer, just ask me.” Those words are not just a slogan. They are an invitation.

We believe some of the most important conversations begin with a simple question. If one shirt leads to one prayer, one encouraging conversation, or one person taking a step closer to Christ, then it has already served a purpose far greater than clothing.

More Than Apparel

As No Vanity 33 grows, our desire is to use the business to serve people in practical ways. We hope to support those facing hardship, encourage families in need, and invest in opportunities that reflect Christ’s love through both words and actions.

We do not claim to have all the answers. We simply want to build a company that honors Christ, treats people with kindness, and reminds the world that God’s Word is living, powerful, and still changing lives today.

Wear your faith. Live with purpose. Point others to Christ.
