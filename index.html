Our Mission

Why No Vanity 33 Exists

No Vanity 33 was founded with one purpose: to point people toward Christ.

In a world that often celebrates self, appearance, and status, we want every design to remind people that true identity is not found in ourselves. It is found in Jesus Christ. The name No Vanity is a reminder that our lives are meant to reflect Him, not ourselves.

Every shirt is created with intention. Our designs are meant to start conversations, encourage faith, and remind both the person wearing them and the people around them that hope is still alive.

One of the things that makes No Vanity 33 unique is that every design includes Scripture. Rather than printing the same verse repeatedly, we intentionally use different passages from the Bible across our apparel. Our hope is that every order places God’s Word into someone’s life in a fresh way. A verse on the back of a shirt may be exactly what someone needed to read on an ordinary day.

You will also notice a simple message on our apparel: “If you need prayer, just ask me.” Those words are not just a slogan. They are an invitation.

As No Vanity 33 grows, our desire is to use the business to serve people in practical ways: helping those facing hardship, encouraging families in need, and reflecting Christ’s love through both words and actions.

Wear your faith. Live with purpose. Point others to Christ.
