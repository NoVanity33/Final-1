Crimson Worm Tee
Price: $25
Scripture: Psalm 22:6

Description:
A teaching tee based on Psalm 22:6 and the crimson worm symbolism.

Why This Design?
Psalm 22:6 says, “But I am a worm, and no man.” The crimson worm has often been used as a picture of sacrifice, scarlet covering, and new life — pointing to the Gospel of Christ.
